Strategic initiatives · 2025–2026

Where the company is leaning in

Themes from earnings, investor materials, and Momentum — framed for discussion, not as gospel.

1

Cloud conversion at scale

Move remaining on-prem customers to Manhattan Active with fixed-price, fixed-timeline conversions — services as the primary engine.

2

Balanced growth mix

Target roughly one-third new logo, one-third conversion, one-third expansion — with dedicated renewals focus into 2026.

3

Agentic AI on Active

Commercial Active Agents, Agent Foundry for customer-built agents, and Marketplace for distribution — embedded across SCE / planning / commerce.

4

Platform & go-to-market scale

Expand Active suite & TAM; grow cloud subscription revenue; invest in global sales/marketing and faster implementation experiences.
